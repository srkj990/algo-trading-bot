# 🧠 1. Moving Average (MA) Strategy

### 📌 Logic

* Calculate:

  * MA20 → short-term trend
  * MA50 → longer-term trend

* Decision:

  * MA20 > MA50 → **BUY**
  * MA20 < MA50 → **SELL**

---

### 🎯 What It Captures

👉 Trend direction

* Short MA crossing above long MA = bullish momentum
* Opposite = bearish momentum

---

### ✅ Works Best In

* Trending markets (strong up/down move)

---

### ❌ Fails In

* Sideways market (frequent false signals)

---

### 🧠 Intuition

> “Follow the trend, don’t predict it”

---

# 🟢 2. RSI (Relative Strength Index)

### 📌 Logic

* RSI range: 0–100
* Decision:

  * RSI < 30 → **BUY (oversold)**
  * RSI > 70 → **SELL (overbought)**

---

### 🎯 What It Captures

👉 Momentum exhaustion

* Price fell too much → likely bounce
* Price rose too much → likely fall

---

### ✅ Works Best In

* Sideways / range-bound markets

---

### ❌ Fails In

* Strong trends (RSI stays high/low for long time)

---

### 🧠 Intuition

> “Too much of anything reverses”

---

# 🟡 3. Breakout Strategy

### 📌 Logic

* Look at last 20 candles:

  * Highest high
  * Lowest low

* Decision:

  * Price breaks above high → **BUY**
  * Price breaks below low → **SELL**

---

### 🎯 What It Captures

👉 New momentum / volatility expansion

---

### ✅ Works Best In

* When market starts moving after consolidation

---

### ❌ Fails In

* Fake breakouts (price breaks and reverses)

---

### 🧠 Intuition

> “Big moves start after quiet periods”

---

# 🟣 4. VWAP Strategy

### 📌 Logic

* VWAP = average price weighted by volume

* Decision:

  * Price > VWAP → **BUY**
  * Price < VWAP → **SELL**

---

### 🎯 What It Captures

👉 Institutional bias

* Above VWAP → buyers in control
* Below VWAP → sellers in control

---

### ✅ Works Best In

* Intraday trading

---

### ❌ Fails In

* Choppy markets near VWAP

---

### 🧠 Intuition

> “Smart money trades around VWAP”

---

# 🟠 5. ORB (Opening Range Breakout)

### 📌 Logic

* Take first 15-minute range:

  * High
  * Low

* Decision:

  * Break above → **BUY**
  * Break below → **SELL**

---

### 🎯 What It Captures

👉 Early market direction

---

### ✅ Works Best In

* Strong opening trends

---

### ❌ Fails In

* Flat openings / fake moves

---

### 🧠 Intuition

> “First move of the day often defines direction”

---

# ⚠️ VERY IMPORTANT TRUTH

No strategy is “best”.

| Market Type | Best Strategy |
| ----------- | ------------- |
| Trending    | MA, Breakout  |
| Sideways    | RSI           |
| Intraday    | VWAP, ORB     |
